Welcome to Hoichoi

Hoichoi in Memari, Bardhaman is a welcoming dining destination that blends traditional flavors with modern touches. Conveniently located near HDFC Bank and the New Bus Stand, it offers a cozy, refined ambiance and attentive service.

Open daily from 12:00 pm to 10:30 pm, the restaurant serves a variety of thoughtfully prepared dishes made with quality ingredients. With its diverse menu, comfortable setting, and commitment to customer satisfaction, Hoichoi provides a memorable dining experience for both locals and visitors.
